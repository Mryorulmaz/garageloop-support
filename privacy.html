GarageLoop Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Data We Collect

  • Account data: email, Apple Sign-In identifiers.
  • Content data: listings, messages, photos you upload.
  • Device data: app version, basic diagnostics and crash reports.
  • Usage data: interactions within the app to improve features.

How We Use Data

  • Provide core features (accounts, listings, messaging).
  • Safety, security, fraud prevention, and abuse handling.
  • Comply with legal obligations.
  • Improve app performance and reliability.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share limited data with service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics) under contractual safeguards.

Your Choices

  • Access, export, or delete your data by emailing support@gmail.com.
  • Disable notifications in iOS Settings at any time.
  • Withdraw consent by deleting your account.

Children

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
