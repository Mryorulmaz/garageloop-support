GarageLoop – Account and Data Deletion

This page explains how you can delete your GarageLoop account and how we handle your data when you request deletion.

Delete your account from the app

If you still have access to the GarageLoop app, you can delete your account directly:

  1. Open the GarageLoop app.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Privacy & Security.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

This will permanently delete your account and associated data (profile information, listings, messages and credits) from our active systems. Some data may be retained for a limited period where required for fraud prevention, abuse detection or legal obligations.

If you can't access the app

If you can no longer access the app, you can request deletion by email:

  1. Send an email from the same address you used to sign up to:
    support@garageloop.com (veya kendi support e‑postan neyse onunla değiştir).
  2. Use the subject line “Delete GarageLoop account”.
  3. Include a short note that you want your account and associated data to be deleted.

We will respond to your request and delete your account and associated data within 30 days, unless we are required to keep certain information for legal reasons.
